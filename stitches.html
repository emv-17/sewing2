Sewing Techniques

this page will explain several different sewing techniques, showing how and when to use them

Running/Straight Stitch

the simplest stitch

  • thread your needle
    • follow french knot tutorial
    • OR tie knot as usual at the end of the thread, doubling for strength and width
  • start at the back, bring needle through the fabric to come out the front
  • bring needle from the front, through fabric again, to the back

Whip Stitch

Good for quick stitching that you don't mind seeing

  • thread needle
  • bring needle from the back of the fabric through it to the front
  • bring needle around, back to the back
  • repeat bringing needle from back to front

Backstitch

Good for seams that need to be strong

  • thread needle
  • bring needle from back to front through the fabric, going only half the stitch desired length
  • Bring needle back through the frabric to the back
  • go throuhg to the front, pass stitch 1 by double the length and go through fabric to the back
  • repeat by going double each stitch length
